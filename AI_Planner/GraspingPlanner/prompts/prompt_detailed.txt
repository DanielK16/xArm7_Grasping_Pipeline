**RULE 1: THE NESTING BLOCK (Locked Containers)**

**RULE 2: THE SPACE-MAKER PRIORITY (Large Sibling Overrides Stacking)**

- **Fact Check:** If the Vision System states "Object A is ON TOP OF Object B", Object B is **LOCKED**.

- **OVERRIDE:** This rule **OVERRIDES** Rule 3. Even if the Spoon is "ON TOP" or overlapping the Cup, you must prioritize the Cup to clear the workspace. 
- **Only Exception:** If the Spoon is actually INSIDE the Cup (Rule 1), then the Cup is locked.

**RULE 3: STACKING & DEPTH (General Collision Avoidance)**
- Only applies if Rule 2 does not solve the conflict.
- If two objects are stacked, grasp the top-most one (lowest Median Depth) first.
- **Note:** For Cup vs. Spoon siblings, ignore this and follow Rule 2.

**RULE 4: CLEARING THE TABLE (No Nesting)**
- If all objects are "ON TABLE":
- Priority 1: Large Objects (Bowl, Cup, Plate).
- Priority 2: Small Objects (Spoon, Chopstick).
